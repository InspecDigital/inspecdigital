About Inspec

WHO

Ashley J. has over 10 years of customer service experience, 5 years including volunteer administrative work with a local non-profit, and is finishing up her Accounting Degree at Portland State University. She pushes the paper along so Inspec Digital can do what it does best: design great websites.

Tony M. started building websites in a one bedroom college apartment while attending Boise State University sometime in the 1990s. After college, he moved to Portland and began a successful web development career while still making and publishing art.

Rick V. is a graphic designer and illustrator, though the currents of the 21st century have pulled him into the web design dimension, where he uses his visual design skills to create successful communications. A well-rounded and hard-working force on any project.

WHAT

Inspec Digital is a full-service digital design and development shop in Portland, Oregon. We are a truly egalitarian business run by a creative team of worker-owners, each with a variety of strengths and experiences.

Whatever your situation, we provide quality services and solutions at affordable rates. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you tell your story.

WHY

Modern, effective websites and custom software are expensive, but we really don't think they should be. At Inspec Digital, we use our skills to bring affordable, quality design and development to working people everywhere.

It doesn't matter if you have unlimited resources, or are on a tight budget, and it doesn't matter if you are a business or a charity. Inspec Digital is committed to providing the very best services within any budget.

It is the historic mission of Inspec Digital to do away with websites that suck.
