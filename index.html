About Inspec

WHO

  • Ashley J. - the books, the numbers and the friendly communications
  • Tony M. - started building websites in a one bedroom college appartment while attending Boise State University sometime in the 1990s. After college, he moved to Portland and began a successful web development career while still making and publishing art.
  • Rick V. - initially a graphic designer and illustrator, but the currents of reality have pulled him into web design where he uses his visual design skills to create successful communications. A well-rounded and hard-working force on any project.

WHAT

Inspec Digital is a full-service digital design and development shop in Portland, Oregon. We are a truly egalitarian business, run by a creative team of worker-owners with a variety of strengths and experiences.

Whatever your situation, we provide quality services and solutions at affordable rates. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you tell your story.

WHY

Modern, effective websites and custom software are expensive, but we don't think they should be. At Inspec Digital, we use our skills to bring affordable, quality design and development to working people everywhere. It doesn't matter if you have unlimited resources, or are on a tight budget, and it doesn't matter if you are a business or a charity. Inspec Digital is commited to providing the very best services within any budget.

It is the historic mission of Inspec Digital to do away with websites that suck.
